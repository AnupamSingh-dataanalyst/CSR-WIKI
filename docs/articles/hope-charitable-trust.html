HOPE Charitable Trust

The HOPE Charitable Trust is a non-governmental organization (NGO) in India that aims to provide support and services to underprivileged communities. Established with the goal of empowering marginalized groups, the trust works towards creating a more equitable society through various initiatives and programs. By focusing on education, healthcare, and economic development, the HOPE Charitable Trust strives to make a positive impact on the lives of those it serves.

Key Details

The HOPE Charitable Trust is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, and is also registered with the Income Tax Department under Section 12A of the Income Tax Act, 1961, allowing it to receive tax-exempt donations. The trust is required to file its annual returns with the Income Tax Department using Form ITR-7 and is also mandated to file its audited accounts with the Registrar of Trusts. The trust's annual income threshold for audit is Rs. 25 lakhs, beyond which it is required to get its accounts audited by a chartered accountant.
