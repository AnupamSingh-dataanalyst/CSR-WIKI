Gurukul Pratishthan

Gurukul Pratishthan is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India that focuses on providing education and skill development to underprivileged children and youth. The organization aims to empower individuals through quality education, vocational training, and community development programs. By doing so, Gurukul Pratishthan strives to create a positive impact on the lives of marginalized communities and contribute to the country's overall social and economic growth.

Key Details

Gurukul Pratishthan operates in accordance with the provisions of the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, and is registered under Section 12A of the Income-tax Act, 1961.
