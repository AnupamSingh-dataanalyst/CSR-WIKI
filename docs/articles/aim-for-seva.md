# AIM For Seva
AIM For Seva is a non-profit organization in India that focuses on providing education and healthcare to underprivileged children. Established in 2000, it aims to create a supportive environment for these children to grow and develop. With a presence in over 15 states across India, AIM For Seva works towards empowering children through various initiatives and programs.

## Key Details
AIM For Seva operates through a network of Chatralayams, which are student hostels that provide free accommodation, food, and education to children. The organization has over 100 Chatralayams across India, catering to more than 5,000 children. AIM For Seva is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, and is exempt under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961, allowing donors to claim tax deductions on their donations.

## Who Needs This
AIM For Seva is relevant to non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and companies operating in the education and healthcare sectors in India. NGOs working with underprivileged children can collaborate with AIM For Seva to expand their reach and impact. Companies can also partner with AIM For Seva as part of their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives, as mandated by Section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Step-by-Step Process
To partner with AIM For Seva, NGOs and companies can follow these steps:
1. Visit the AIM For Seva website and fill out the online partnership form.
2. Submit the required documents, including registration certificates and annual reports.
3. Discuss the partnership proposal with the AIM For Seva team, outlining the scope of work and expected outcomes.
4. Sign a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) outlining the terms and conditions of the partnership.

## Important Deadlines / Penalties
There are no specific deadlines or penalties associated with partnering with AIM For Seva. However, companies must ensure that their CSR spending is in compliance with the Companies Act, 2013, and file their CSR reports with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs through the MCA21 portal.
