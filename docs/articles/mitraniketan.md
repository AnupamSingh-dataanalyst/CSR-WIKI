# Mitraniketan
Mitraniketan is a pioneering organization in India that has been working towards the development of rural communities since its inception in 1956. Founded by Viswanathan, it is based in Vellanad, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, and focuses on empowering rural communities through education, skill development, and sustainable livelihood initiatives. Mitraniketan's approach is centered around community participation and self-reliance, aiming to create a holistic and equitable society.

## Key Details
Mitraniketan was established with the objective of promoting rural development and self-sufficiency. The organization operates through various programs, including education, vocational training, and community development initiatives. Some key details about Mitraniketan include:
- Registration under the Travancore-Cochin Literary, Scientific and Charitable Societies Registration Act, 1955
- Emphasis on community-led initiatives and participatory approaches
- Collaboration with government agencies, NGOs, and corporate organizations to leverage resources and expertise
