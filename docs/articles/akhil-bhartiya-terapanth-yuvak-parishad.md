# Akhil Bhartiya Terapanth Yuvak Parishad
Akhil Bhartiya Terapanth Yuvak Parishad is a youth organization associated with the Terapanth Jain sect, focusing on the spiritual, social, and cultural development of its members. Established with the aim of promoting Jain values and community service, it operates under the guidance of the Terapanth Jain community's principles. The organization engages in various activities, including educational programs, social service initiatives, and cultural events, to foster personal growth and community involvement among its members.

## Key Details
The Akhil Bhartiya Terapanth Yuvak Parishad has its presence across India, with numerous local chapters and a central governing body that oversees its operations. The organization's activities are designed for youths, typically between the ages of 15 and 35, who are affiliated with the Terapanth Jain community. Members participate in annual conventions, training camps, and community service projects, which are planned and executed by the organization's leadership team.
