Goonj (NGO)

Goonj is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India that focuses on addressing the basic needs of the poor and marginalized communities. Founded in 1999 by Anshu Gupta, Goonj works on various issues such as disaster relief, education, and rural development. The organization is known for its innovative approach to addressing social and economic problems, and has received numerous awards and recognition for its work.

Key Details

Goonj was registered as a non-profit organization under the Societies Registration Act of 1860 and is also registered under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The organization has a presence in over 25 states across India and has worked with thousands of communities, providing them with clothing, education, and other essential resources.
