# Indian National Congress
The Indian National Congress is a major political party in India that played a significant role in the country's struggle for independence from British rule. Founded in 1885, it is one of the oldest and largest political parties in India, with a rich history of leadership and social reform. The party has been instrumental in shaping India's political landscape and has produced several notable leaders, including Mahatma Gandhi and Jawaharlal Nehru.

## Key Details
The Indian National Congress has a complex organizational structure, with a president, vice-president, and various committees and departments. The party is headquartered in New Delhi and has a presence in all states and union territories of India. The party's membership is open to all Indian citizens above the age of 18, and it has a large following across the country.
