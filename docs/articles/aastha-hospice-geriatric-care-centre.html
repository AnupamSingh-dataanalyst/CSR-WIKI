Aastha Hospice & Geriatric Care Centre

Aastha Hospice & Geriatric Care Centre is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing palliative care and support to terminally ill patients and elderly individuals in need. The centre aims to improve the quality of life of its patients through comprehensive medical, emotional, and spiritual care. By offering a range of services, including medical care, counseling, and recreational activities, Aastha Hospice & Geriatric Care Centre strives to make a positive impact on the lives of its patients and their families.

Key Details

Aastha Hospice & Geriatric Care Centre is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, and is exempt from income tax under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The centre has a capacity to accommodate 20 patients and provides services such as doctor consultations, nursing care, physiotherapy, and occupational therapy. The centre also offers counseling services to patients and their families, and has a team of trained volunteers who provide emotional support and companionship to the patients.
