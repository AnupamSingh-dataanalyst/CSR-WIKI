# Manav Utthan Sewa Samiti
Manav Utthan Sewa Samiti (M USS) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India, working towards the upliftment of marginalized communities. Established with the aim of promoting social and economic development, M USS focuses on education, healthcare, and empowerment of underprivileged groups. The organization operates under the principles of compassion, equality, and justice, striving to create a more equitable society.

## Key Details
Manav Utthan Sewa Samiti is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, and is also registered with the Income Tax Department under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The organization is required to file annual returns with the Registrar of Societies and the Income Tax Department, using forms such as Form 10B and Form ITR-7. M USS is also eligible to receive foreign contributions, having obtained a Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act (FCRA) registration.
