Pingalwara

Pingalwara is a charitable organization based in Amritsar, India, that provides care and shelter to the homeless, disabled, and abandoned people. Founded in 1947 by Bhagat Puran Singh, Pingalwara has been working tirelessly to serve the underprivileged and marginalized sections of society. The organization runs various facilities, including old-age homes, orphanages, and rehabilitation centers, to provide food, shelter, and medical care to those in need.

Key Details

Pingalwara is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, and is exempt from income tax under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The organization has a strong presence in Punjab and has been recognized for its outstanding work in the field of social service. Pingalwara has a team of dedicated staff and volunteers who work together to manage its various facilities and programs, which include a 25-acre campus with accommodation for over 1,500 residents.
