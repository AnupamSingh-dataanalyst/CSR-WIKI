Ajmal Foundation

The Ajmal Foundation is a non-profit organization based in India that focuses on promoting education, healthcare, and social welfare. Established by the Ajmal family, the foundation aims to contribute to the development of society through various philanthropic activities. With a strong commitment to giving back to the community, the Ajmal Foundation has been actively involved in supporting initiatives that benefit the underprivileged and marginalized sections of society.

Key Details

The Ajmal Foundation was founded in 2005 by Badruddin Ajmal, a renowned entrepreneur and philanthropist. The foundation has implemented numerous projects and programs in the areas of education, healthcare, and women's empowerment, benefiting thousands of people across India. Some of the key initiatives include the establishment of schools, colleges, and hospitals, as well as the provision of scholarships and vocational training to underprivileged students.
