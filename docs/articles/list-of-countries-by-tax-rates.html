List of countries by tax rates

Key Details

The list of countries by tax rates includes corporate tax rates, individual income tax rates, and other taxes such as value-added tax (VAT) and goods and services tax (GST). The tax rates vary significantly across countries, with some countries having a flat tax rate while others have a progressive tax system.
