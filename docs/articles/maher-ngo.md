# Maher (NGO)
Maher is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India that works towards the empowerment of women, children, and marginalized communities. Founded in 1997 by Sister Lucy Kurien, Maher aims to provide a safe and supportive environment for those in need, promoting social justice and human rights. With its headquarters in Pune, Maharashtra, Maher has expanded its reach to various parts of the country, addressing issues such as domestic violence, child abuse, and poverty.

## Key Details
Maher is registered under the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950, and is also registered with the Income Tax Department under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The NGO has a strong focus on community development, education, and healthcare, and has established several centers and programs to cater to the needs of vulnerable populations. Maher's annual budget is around Rs. 5 crores, which is utilized to support its various initiatives and projects.

## Who Needs This
Maher's services and support are primarily targeted towards marginalized communities, including women, children, and families affected by poverty, violence, and social injustice. NGOs and companies working in the areas of women's empowerment, child welfare, and community development may find it beneficial to collaborate with Maher or support its initiatives. Additionally, individuals and organizations looking to make a positive impact in the lives of vulnerable populations may consider partnering with or donating to Maher.

## Step-by-Step Process
To partner with or support Maher, the following steps can be taken:
1. Visit the Maher website to learn more about its initiatives and programs.
2. Contact the Maher team to discuss potential collaboration opportunities or to inquire about donation procedures.
3. Fill out the necessary forms, such as the donation form or partnership proposal, and submit them to the Maher office.
4. Maher's team will review the proposal or donation and respond with further instructions or information.

## Important Deadlines / Penalties
As a registered NGO, Maher is required to file annual reports and returns with the Income Tax Department and the Charity Commissioner's office. The deadlines for these filings are typically as follows:
- Income Tax Return: 30th September of each year
- Annual Report to the Charity Commissioner: 31st December of each year
Failure to comply with these deadlines may result in penalties and fines, as per the relevant laws and regulations.
