AVP Research Foundation

The AVP Research Foundation is a non-profit organization based in India that focuses on promoting scientific research and education in various fields, including Ayurveda, Yoga, and other traditional Indian systems of medicine. Established with the goal of advancing knowledge and understanding in these areas, the foundation supports research initiatives, publishes scholarly articles, and provides educational resources. By fostering a deeper understanding of traditional Indian systems of medicine, the AVP Research Foundation aims to contribute to the development of holistic and integrative healthcare approaches.

Key Details

The AVP Research Foundation is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, and is exempt from income tax under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.
