# AVP Research Foundation
The AVP Research Foundation is a non-profit organization based in India that focuses on promoting scientific research and education in various fields, including Ayurveda, Yoga, and other traditional Indian systems of medicine. Established with the goal of advancing knowledge and understanding in these areas, the foundation supports research initiatives, publishes scholarly articles, and provides educational resources. By fostering a deeper understanding of traditional Indian systems of medicine, the AVP Research Foundation aims to contribute to the development of holistic and integrative healthcare approaches.

## Key Details
The AVP Research Foundation is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, and is exempt from income tax under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The foundation has a governing board that oversees its activities, and it receives funding from various sources, including donations and grants. To receive funding, researchers and institutions must submit proposals that align with the foundation's objectives, using the prescribed application form, which can be downloaded from the foundation's website.

## Who Needs This
The AVP Research Foundation's funding opportunities and resources are available to NGOs, research institutions, and individual researchers working in the fields of Ayurveda, Yoga, and other traditional Indian systems of medicine. Companies involved in the development of herbal products, Ayurvedic medicines, and other related industries may also benefit from collaborating with the foundation or accessing its research findings. Additionally, academic institutions and universities with programs in traditional Indian systems of medicine may find the foundation's resources and funding opportunities relevant to their work.

## Step-by-Step Process
To apply for funding or access resources from the AVP Research Foundation, researchers and institutions can follow these steps:
1. Visit the foundation's website to review the eligibility criteria and funding priorities.
2. Download and complete the application form, ensuring that all required documents and information are included.
3. Submit the application, along with any supporting materials, to the foundation's office by the specified deadline.
4. The foundation's governing board will review the application and make a decision regarding funding or resource allocation.
