Being Human Foundation

The Being Human Foundation is a charitable trust founded by Bollywood actor Salman Khan in 2007, with the primary objective of providing education and healthcare to the underprivileged. The foundation aims to make a positive impact on the lives of people in need, with a focus on supporting children and the elderly. Through its various initiatives, the foundation has been working towards creating a better future for the marginalized sections of society.

Key Details

The Being Human Foundation is registered under the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950, and is also registered with the Income Tax Department under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961, allowing donors to claim tax exemptions. The foundation has partnered with various organizations, including the Government of Maharashtra, to implement its projects. Some of the key initiatives of the foundation include providing educational support to over 200 children, conducting health camps, and supporting disaster relief efforts.
