Disable Welfare Trust of India

The Disable Welfare Trust of India is a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving the lives of people with disabilities in India. The trust works towards providing education, employment, and healthcare opportunities to individuals with disabilities, promoting their overall well-being and empowerment. By supporting the trust, individuals and organizations can contribute to creating a more inclusive and equitable society for people with disabilities.

Key Details

The Disable Welfare Trust of India is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, and is eligible to receive tax-deductible donations under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The trust has a unique registration number and is required to file annual returns with the Income Tax Department using Form ITR-7. The trust's objectives include providing financial assistance, educational support, and medical aid to people with disabilities, with a focus on promoting their economic and social independence.

The Disable Welfare Trust of India is relevant to non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and companies that work with people with disabilities or aim to support disability-related causes.
