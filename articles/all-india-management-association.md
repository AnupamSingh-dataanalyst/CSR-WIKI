# All India Management Association
The All India Management Association (AIMA) is a national apex body of the management profession in India, with the aim of promoting management capabilities to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the corporate sector. Established in 1957, AIMA has been contributing to management development in the country, focusing on building professional management in the corporate sector and other areas. AIMA works closely with the government, academicians, and other stakeholders to achieve its objectives.

## Key Details
AIMA is headquartered in New Delhi and has a presence in major cities across India, with over 30 local management associations affiliated to it. AIMA offers various services, including training and development programs, research and publications, and networking opportunities for its members. To become a member of AIMA, organizations and individuals need to fill out the membership application form, which can be downloaded from the AIMA website or obtained from the AIMA office.

## Who Needs This
AIMA membership is open to individuals and organizations interested in management development, including companies, NGOs, academic institutions, and government agencies. NGOs and companies involved in corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives, as mandated by the Companies Act 2013, can benefit from AIMA's expertise and resources in areas such as leadership development, strategy, and sustainability.

## Step-by-Step Process
To become an AIMA member, the step-by-step process involves:
1. Visiting the AIMA website to download the membership application form or obtaining it from the AIMA office.
2. Filling out the application form with the required details, including organization or individual profile, contact information, and membership category.
3. Submitting the application form along with the required documents and payment, which can be made online or offline.

## Important Deadlines / Penalties
There are no specific deadlines for AIMA membership application, and the process is open throughout the year. However, members are required to renew their membership annually, and failure to do so may result in loss of membership benefits and privileges.
