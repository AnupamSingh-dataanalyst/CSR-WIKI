DAISY Forum of India

The DAISY Forum of India is a non-profit organization that aims to promote the adoption of DAISY (Digital Accessible Information System) standards for producing accessible books and other reading materials for people with print disabilities. Established in 2005, the forum works towards creating a barrier-free environment for individuals with visual impairments, dyslexia, and other reading disabilities. The DAISY Forum of India is a member of the DAISY Consortium, an international organization that sets standards for accessible digital content.

Key Details

The DAISY Forum of India has partnered with various organizations, including the National Institute for the Visually Handicapped (NIVH) and the National Centre for Promotion of Employment for Disabled People (NCPEDP), to promote the use of DAISY standards in India. The forum provides training and support for the production of DAISY digital talking books, which can be played on specialized devices or smartphones with DAISY software. As of 2022, the forum has produced over 10,000 DAISY books in various Indian languages, including Hindi, English, and Tamil.

Who Needs This

The DAISY Forum of India's services are primarily targeted towards individuals with print disabilities, including those with visual impairments, dyslexia, and other reading disabilities. Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and companies that work with people with disabilities, educational institutions, and libraries can also benefit from the forum's services. Additionally, publishers and authors who want to make their books accessible to a wider audience can partner with the DAISY Forum of India to produce DAISY digital talking books.

Step-by-Step Process

To access the DAISY Forum of India's services, individuals and organizations can follow these steps: 1. Register on the DAISY Forum of India's website to become a member. 2. Fill out the application form for DAISY book production, providing details about the book and the target audience. 3. Submit the application form and wait for approval from the DAISY Forum of India. 4. Once approved, the DAISY Forum of India will produce the DAISY digital talking book and provide it to the applicant.

Important Deadlines / Penalties
