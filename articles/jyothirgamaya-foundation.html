Jyothirgamaya Foundation

The Jyothirgamaya Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India that focuses on empowering visually impaired individuals by providing them with education, training, and employment opportunities. Founded with the aim of promoting inclusivity and accessibility, the foundation works towards creating a supportive environment for the visually impaired to thrive. Through its various initiatives, the Jyothirgamaya Foundation strives to bridge the gap between the visually impaired and the rest of society.

Key Details

The Jyothirgamaya Foundation was established in 2010 and is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, and the Foreign Contribution Regulation Act, 2010. The foundation operates primarily in the state of Kerala, with its headquarters in Thiruvananthapuram. It has a team of dedicated professionals, including special educators, counselors, and support staff, who work together to provide comprehensive support to visually impaired individuals. The foundation's programs include Braille education, computer training, vocational skills development, and placement services.

Who Needs This

The Jyothirgamaya Foundation's services are primarily targeted towards visually impaired individuals, particularly those from underprivileged backgrounds. NGOs and companies that work with people with disabilities, as well as those that focus on education, employment, and social inclusion, may find it beneficial to collaborate with the Jyothirgamaya Foundation. Additionally, corporate social responsibility (CSR) teams of companies that aim to support disability-related causes may consider partnering with the foundation to support its initiatives.

Step-by-Step Process

To collaborate with the Jyothirgamaya Foundation, interested NGOs or companies can follow these steps: 1. Visit the foundation's website to learn more about its programs and initiatives. 2. Contact the foundation's team to discuss potential partnership opportunities. 3. Submit a proposal outlining the proposed collaboration, including the objectives, scope, and expected outcomes. 4. Review and sign a memorandum of understanding (MoU) to formalize the partnership.

Important Deadlines / Penalties

As the Jyothirgamaya Foundation is a voluntary organization, there are no specific deadlines or penalties associated with its programs. However, NGOs and companies that collaborate with the foundation are expected to adhere to the terms and conditions outlined in the MoU and to report on their activities and progress in a timely manner.
