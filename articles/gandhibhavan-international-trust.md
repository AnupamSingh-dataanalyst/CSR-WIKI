# Gandhibhavan International Trust
The Gandhibhavan International Trust is a non-profit organization established to promote the principles of Mahatma Gandhi and foster international understanding and cooperation. The trust aims to provide a platform for cultural exchange, education, and community development, with a focus on promoting peace, non-violence, and sustainable living. By supporting various initiatives and projects, the Gandhibhavan International Trust strives to make a positive impact on society and inspire individuals to work towards creating a more harmonious and equitable world.

## Key Details
The Gandhibhavan International Trust is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, and is exempt from income tax under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The trust has a governing board comprising prominent individuals from various fields, including education, social work, and the arts. The trust's activities include organizing seminars, workshops, and cultural events, as well as providing support to grassroots organizations and community development projects.
