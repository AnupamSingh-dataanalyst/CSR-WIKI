# Prabartak Sangha
Prabartak Sangha is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India, focused on promoting social welfare and community development. The organization works towards empowering marginalized communities, particularly women and children, through various initiatives and programs. With a strong commitment to creating positive change, Prabartak Sangha has been actively engaged in addressing social issues and promoting sustainable development.

## Key Details
Prabartak Sangha is registered under the West Bengal Societies Registration Act, 1960, and is also registered with the National Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Hub, a portal launched by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. The organization has a dedicated team of professionals and volunteers working towards its mission, and it has implemented various projects in the areas of education, healthcare, and livelihood development. To ensure transparency and accountability, Prabartak Sangha files its annual returns with the Registrar of Societies and also submits its audited financial statements to the Income Tax Department.
