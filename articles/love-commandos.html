Love Commandos

Love Commandos is a non-governmental organization (NGO) in India that provides assistance and protection to couples who face threats and violence due to their decision to marry against the wishes of their families or society. The organization was founded in 2010 by Sanjoy Sachdev, and it has since helped thousands of couples in distress. Love Commandos operates a helpline and provides shelter, legal aid, and counseling to couples in need.

Key Details

Love Commandos has a network of volunteers and activists across India, with a presence in over 200 districts. The organization receives an average of 100 calls per day on its helpline, and it has helped over 30,000 couples since its inception. Love Commandos also works with the police and other authorities to provide protection to couples who face threats from their families or community. The organization is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, and it files its annual returns with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs through the Form 20-B.

Who Needs This

Love Commandos' services are available to any couple who faces threats or violence due to their decision to marry against the wishes of their families or society. This includes couples from different castes, religions, or communities, as well as those who are in a same-sex relationship. NGOs and companies that work on issues related to human rights, women's empowerment, and social justice may also find Love Commandos' work relevant to their own mission and goals.

Step-by-Step Process

Couples who need assistance from Love Commandos can follow these steps: 1. Call the Love Commandos helpline to report their situation and seek help. 2. Provide details about their situation, including the nature of the threats they face and their location. 3. Love Commandos will assess their situation and provide guidance on the next steps to take. 4. If necessary, Love Commandos will provide shelter, legal aid, and counseling to the couple.

Important Deadlines / Penalties

There are no specific deadlines or penalties associated with seeking help from Love Commandos. However, couples who face threats or violence should seek help as soon as possible to ensure their safety.
