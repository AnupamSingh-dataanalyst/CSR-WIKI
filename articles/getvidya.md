# GetVidya
GetVidya is an online platform designed to facilitate the implementation of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives in India, particularly in the education sector. It aims to bridge the gap between companies looking to fulfill their CSR obligations and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) or educational institutions seeking funding for social causes. By providing a structured approach to CSR project implementation, GetVidya simplifies the process for both corporations and NGOs.

## Key Details
GetVidya operates under the guidelines of the Companies Act, 2013, which mandates that companies with a certain net worth, turnover, or net profit must spend at least 2% of their average net profits on CSR activities. The platform focuses on projects related to education, including infrastructure development, digital literacy, and skill development. Companies can choose from a variety of projects listed on the GetVidya platform, with each project detailing its objectives, budget, and expected outcomes.

## Who Needs This
GetVidya is primarily useful for companies that fall under the CSR mandate as per the Companies Act, 2013. These include companies with a net worth of Rs. 500 crore or more, a turnover of Rs. 1,000 crore or more, or a net profit of Rs. 5 crore or more. NGOs and educational institutions looking for funding for their social projects can also benefit from listing their projects on the GetVidya platform. Additionally, CSR foundations, philanthropic organizations, and individuals interested in supporting educational causes can utilize GetVidya to find and support worthy projects.

## Step-by-Step Process
To use GetVidya, companies and NGOs can follow these steps:
1. Registration: Companies and NGOs register on the GetVidya platform, providing necessary details about their organization and, for NGOs, the projects they wish to list.
2. Project Listing: NGOs list their projects on the platform, including project details, budgets, and expected outcomes.
3. Project Selection: Companies browse through listed projects, select those that align with their CSR goals, and initiate the funding process.
4. Project Implementation: Once funding is secured, NGOs implement the projects, with GetVidya providing oversight and ensuring that projects are executed as planned.
5. Reporting: NGOs submit regular progress reports, and upon project completion, a final report detailing the outcomes and impact of the project.

## Important Deadlines / Penalties
Companies must ensure that they spend the mandated 2% of their average net profits on CSR activities within the financial year. Failure to comply with the CSR spending requirement can result in penalties, including fines and potential legal action. The CSR expenditure must be reported in the company's annual report on CSR, which is filed with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs through the MCA21 portal.
