Economy of India

The Economy of India is a complex and diverse system that has undergone significant transformations over the years, driven by government policies, technological advancements, and global market trends. As the fifth-largest economy in the world, India's economic growth has been steadily increasing, with a focus on sustainable development and social welfare. The NITI Aayog plays a crucial role in shaping the country's economic policies, promoting cooperative federalism, and fostering economic development.

Key Details

The Indian economy is characterized by a mix of public and private sectors, with a growing services sector, a significant agricultural sector, and a rapidly expanding industrial sector. The country has a large and diverse population, with a growing middle class, and a rising GDP, which has reached over $2.7 trillion. The government has introduced various initiatives, such as the Make in India program, the Digital India initiative, and the Start-Up India scheme, to boost economic growth, promote entrepreneurship, and create jobs.
