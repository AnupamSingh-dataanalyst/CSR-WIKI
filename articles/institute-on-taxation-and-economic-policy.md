# Institute on Taxation and Economic Policy
The Institute on Taxation and Economic Policy (ITEP) is a non-profit, non-partisan tax policy organization that provides data-driven analysis of tax policies at the federal, state, and local levels in the United States. ITEP's work focuses on issues related to tax fairness, sustainability, and economic growth, with the goal of promoting more equitable and effective tax systems. Although based in the US, ITEP's research and principles can inform discussions on taxation and economic policy globally, including in India, where income tax laws and policies are governed by the Income-tax Act, 1961, and other regulations.

## Key Details
ITEP was founded in 1980 and is based in Washington, D.C. The organization uses its expertise in tax policy analysis to provide research and commentary on a wide range of issues, including federal and state tax reform, tax credits for low- and moderate-income families, and the impact of tax policies on different socioeconomic groups. ITEP's work involves analyzing tax data and policies, often using its own microsimulation tax model to estimate the effects of tax law changes on various groups and the broader economy.
