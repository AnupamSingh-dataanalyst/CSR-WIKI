Tax Justice Network

The Tax Justice Network is an independent organization that works to promote tax justice and fairness globally, with a focus on addressing issues related to tax evasion, avoidance, and corruption. It aims to create a more equitable and transparent tax system, ensuring that governments have the resources needed to provide essential public services. The network collaborates with various stakeholders, including governments, civil society organizations, and individuals, to achieve its objectives.

Key Details

The Tax Justice Network was founded in 2003 and is headquartered in the United Kingdom. It has a global presence, with partners and members from over 100 countries. The network's work is guided by several key principles, including the need for transparency, accountability, and fairness in tax systems.
