# Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation
The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation is a philanthropic organization founded by Bill Gates and Melinda French Gates in 2000, with the primary goal of enhancing healthcare and reducing extreme poverty globally. The foundation has been actively involved in various initiatives in India, collaborating with the government, NGOs, and private sector organizations to achieve its objectives. With a vast array of programs and investments, the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation plays a significant role in supporting India's development agenda, including initiatives aligned with the NITI Aayog's vision for transformative growth.

## Key Details
The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation has committed significant resources to India, with investments exceeding $1.5 billion since its inception. The foundation's initiatives in India focus on areas such as health, sanitation, and agriculture, with a strong emphasis on supporting marginalized communities. Key programs include the foundation's work on vaccine delivery, maternal and child health, and sanitation, often implemented in partnership with state governments and local NGOs.
