# Chavara Cultural Centre
The Chavara Cultural Centre is a cultural institution dedicated to promoting and preserving the art, literature, and heritage of Kerala, India. Established in memory of Saint Kuriakose Elias Chavara, the centre aims to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation of Kerala's rich cultural diversity. Through various programs and activities, the centre seeks to promote cultural exchange, education, and community engagement.

## Key Details
The Chavara Cultural Centre is located in Kottayam, Kerala, and offers a range of cultural activities, including traditional dance and music performances, art exhibitions, and literary events. The centre also provides training and workshops in traditional Kerala arts, such as Kathakali and Mohiniyattam. Additionally, the centre has a library and archive section, which houses a collection of rare books, manuscripts, and documents related to Kerala's history and culture.
