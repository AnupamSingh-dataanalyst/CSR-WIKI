ISKCON Youth Forum Ahmedabad

The ISKCON Youth Forum Ahmedabad is a spiritual and cultural organization that aims to inspire and empower the youth of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, through the teachings of Krishna consciousness. As part of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), the forum provides a platform for young people to engage in spiritual growth, community service, and personal development. The organization is dedicated to promoting the values of spirituality, selflessness, and compassion among the youth.

Key Details

The ISKCON Youth Forum Ahmedabad was established in 1983 and has since grown to become one of the largest and most active youth forums in the country. The organization has over 5,000 members and conducts regular programs, including spiritual lectures, cultural events, and community service activities. The forum is led by a team of experienced devotees who provide guidance and support to the members.
