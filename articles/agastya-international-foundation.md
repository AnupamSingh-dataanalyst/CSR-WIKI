# Agastya International Foundation
Agastya International Foundation is a non-profit organization that aims to transform education and empower disadvantaged children in India through hands-on, experiential learning. Founded in 1999, the foundation has been working towards improving the quality of education in rural and underprivileged areas. With a focus on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education, Agastya International Foundation has been making a significant impact on the lives of thousands of children across the country.

## Key Details
Agastya International Foundation has reached out to over 10 million children and 250,000 teachers across 18 states in India, providing them with innovative and interactive learning experiences. The foundation operates through a network of mobile labs, science centers, and digital platforms, offering a range of programs and resources to support education and skill development. Some of the key initiatives of the foundation include the Agastya Mobile Lab, which travels to remote areas to provide hands-on science education, and the Agastya Science Center, which offers a range of interactive exhibits and activities.
