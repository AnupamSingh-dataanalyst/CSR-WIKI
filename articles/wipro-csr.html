Wipro CSR

Wipro's Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives focus on creating a positive impact on society and the environment. The company's CSR efforts are primarily directed towards education, ecology, and community development, aiming to make a meaningful difference in the lives of people and the planet. Through its CSR programs, Wipro strives to contribute to the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and aligns its initiatives with the Indian government's priorities.

Key Details

Wipro's CSR policy is guided by the Companies Act, 2013, and the CSR Rules, 2014. The company spends at least 2% of its average net profits made during the three immediately preceding financial years on CSR activities. Wipro's CSR focus areas include: * Education: promoting quality education, skill development, and vocational training * Ecology: conserving natural resources, reducing carbon footprint, and promoting sustainable practices * Community: supporting community development projects, disaster relief, and healthcare initiatives Wipro's CSR initiatives are implemented through the Wipro Foundation, which is a separate entity established by the company to oversee its CSR activities.
