Mitraniketan

Mitraniketan is a pioneering organization in India that has been working towards the development of rural communities since its inception in 1956. Founded by Viswanathan, it is based in Vellanad, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, and focuses on empowering rural communities through education, skill development, and sustainable livelihood initiatives. Mitraniketan's approach is centered around community participation and self-reliance, aiming to create a holistic and equitable society.

Mitraniketan was established with the objective of promoting rural development and self-sufficiency.
