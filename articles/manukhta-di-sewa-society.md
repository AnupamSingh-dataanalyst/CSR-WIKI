# Manukhta Di Sewa Society
Manukhta Di Sewa Society is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India, dedicated to providing humanitarian aid and social services to underprivileged communities. The society focuses on promoting education, healthcare, and economic empowerment among marginalized groups, with the aim of creating a more equitable and just society. By leveraging donations, grants, and volunteer efforts, Manukhta Di Sewa Society strives to make a positive impact on the lives of those in need.

## Key Details
Manukhta Di Sewa Society is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, and is also registered with the Income Tax Department under Section 12A of the Income Tax Act, 1961, allowing it to receive tax-exempt donations. The society is required to file annual returns with the Registrar of Societies and the Income Tax Department, using forms such as Form 10B and Form 10BB. Additionally, the society must maintain a minimum corpus fund of ₹1 lakh to ensure its financial sustainability.

## Who Needs This
Manukhta Di Sewa Society's services and resources are primarily targeted towards underprivileged communities, including rural villages, urban slums, and marginalized groups such as women, children, and minorities. NGOs and companies operating in the social sector, particularly those focused on education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, may find it beneficial to collaborate with or support Manukhta Di Sewa Society. This includes organizations registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013, as well as those registered under the Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act, 2010.

## Step-by-Step Process
To receive support or collaborate with Manukhta Di Sewa Society, interested NGOs or companies can follow these steps:
1. Visit the society's website or office to learn more about its mission, objectives, and ongoing projects.
2. Submit a proposal outlining the potential collaboration or support required, including details on the target community, project goals, and budget.
3. The society's management committee will review the proposal and respond with feedback or a decision within 30 days.
4. Upon approval, the parties can finalize the terms of collaboration, including funding, resource allocation, and project timelines.

## Important Deadlines / Penalties
Manukhta Di Sewa Society is required to file its annual returns with the Registrar of Societies and the Income Tax Department by the 31st of December each year. Failure to comply with this deadline may result in penalties, including a fine of ₹10,000 or more, as well as potential cancellation of the society's registration.
