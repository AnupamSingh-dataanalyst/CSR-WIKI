# Wipro CSR
Wipro's Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives focus on creating a positive impact on society and the environment. The company's CSR efforts are primarily directed towards education, ecology, and community development, aiming to make a meaningful difference in the lives of people and the planet. Through its CSR programs, Wipro strives to contribute to the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and aligns its initiatives with the Indian government's priorities.

## Key Details
Wipro's CSR policy is guided by the Companies Act, 2013, and the CSR Rules, 2014. The company spends at least 2% of its average net profits made during the three immediately preceding financial years on CSR activities. Wipro's CSR focus areas include:
* Education: promoting quality education, skill development, and vocational training
* Ecology: conserving natural resources, reducing carbon footprint, and promoting sustainable practices
* Community: supporting community development projects, disaster relief, and healthcare initiatives
Wipro's CSR initiatives are implemented through the Wipro Foundation, which is a separate entity established by the company to oversee its CSR activities.

## Who Needs This
Wipro's CSR initiatives are relevant to NGOs, educational institutions, and community organizations that work in the areas of education, ecology, and community development. Companies that are looking to collaborate with Wipro on CSR projects or seeking funding for their social initiatives can also benefit from understanding Wipro's CSR focus areas and priorities. Additionally, CSR managers, grant writers, and NGO professionals who are interested in partnering with Wipro can use this information to explore potential collaboration opportunities.

## Step-by-Step Process
To partner with Wipro on CSR initiatives, NGOs and companies can follow these steps:
1. Review Wipro's CSR policy and focus areas to ensure alignment with their own goals and objectives.
2. Submit a proposal outlining the project's objectives, scope, and budget.
3. Wait for review and approval from Wipro's CSR team.
4. Collaborate with Wipro's team to implement the project and monitor its progress.

## Important Deadlines / Penalties
Wipro's CSR spending is subject to the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013, and the CSR Rules, 2014. The company is required to spend at least 2% of its average net profits on CSR activities every year. Failure to comply with the CSR spending requirements can result in penalties, including fines and reputational damage.
